About Us

The Worcester EMS Relief Association (WEMSRA) is a nonprofit 501(c)(3) based in Worcester, MA. We are dedicated to supporting Worcester EMS professionals during times of personal or professional hardship. Founded by local EMS personnel, our mission is to give back to those who serve our community day and night.

What We Do

  • Provide financial and emotional support to EMS personnel in need
  • Host awards and recognition events
  • Raise funds through merchandise and donation campaigns
  • Honor veterans and community partners
  • Support memorials and line-of-duty death commemorations

Why It Matters

EMS professionals face high-stress situations daily. When they're the ones who need help—whether due to illness, injury, or loss—WEMSRA steps in to support them and their families. Our mission is grounded in compassion, respect, and the belief that no first responder should face hardship alone.

Get Involved

We welcome community support! You can help by donating or participating in our events. We often raise money by selling commemorative patches and gear. Volunteers and sponsors are also essential to our continued success—reach out to see how you can contribute.
